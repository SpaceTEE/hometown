About Us

At some point in every individual’s life, you might feel that you need a little break for a little while. Some might not want to spend a lot of expenses traveling to a different country. If you are a person who lives or is visiting within Canad and do not mind being close to the mountains. Banff is a beautiful place to experience, seeing the scenery and the town. Banff, a small community built in a national park has many attractions to get involved in.

The town of Banff was the first municipality in Canada to be incorporated inside a national park in the 1990s. In 1885, Banff was the first and the world’s third national park to be established during that time. Home to 8,000 residents, the town offers many establishments to tourists such as campgrounds within the park, restaurants, hotels and gift shops. This little hideaway of a community within the rocky mountain is one of Canada’s national treasures to behold.
